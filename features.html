Antares runs silently as you browse. No manual input, no setup. Every Solana address you encounter triggers a full multi-source scan in seconds.

Core Features

Critical Actors

Who holds what, in 3 cards.

Dev wallet reputation (prior tokens launched, prior rugs), insider sniper position, coordinated cluster size and coordination score. Each card surfaces the structural risk vector behind the score, not just a number.

Insider Watch (live heatmap)

See top wallets dumping in real time.

Top 12 wallets in a colour-coded tile grid. Each tile shows the wallet’s 6h delta and what they’re doing (Accumulating · Holding · Reducing · Selling · Dumping). Dev wallet flagged with a purple ring. Catches what static screenshots miss.

Buy/Sell Flow (per window)

Money in vs money out, in real time.

Pressure gauge with ACCUMULATION / DISTRIBUTION / BALANCED banner driven by buy/sell transaction counts and volume across 5min / 1h / 6h / 24h windows. Lean indicator + per-window mini cards. Tells you the direction before you click buy.

Wash Volume Score

Is the volume real, or fake?

0-100 wash score derived from average trade size relative to liquidity + buy/sell symmetry. Tiny trades cycling against a thin LP score high; healthy organic trading scores low. Real volume estimate alongside the reported headline. No more falling for puffed numbers.

7-Layer Consensus

No single source can lie alone.

DexScreener, RugCheck, GoPlus, Helius, Solscan, the in-house Chart engine and a CrossValidation layer that catches inter-source disagreements. Geometric mean of all 7 layers means a single false-clean source can’t override real risk — and a single false-positive can’t flip a healthy token to RUG.

What Else You Get
Source Breakdown

One row per upstream

RugCheck, Helius, Solscan, Chart Engine, DexScreener — each with its own derived verdict (OK / Risk / Flagged / N/A) and a one-line note. Open the accordion at the bottom of any analysis to see exactly which source said what. Forensic provenance, not a black-box score.

Sniper Map

Did the launch bots cash out yet?

Retention bar showing how much of supply the top 10 holders still control vs how much has distributed. Combined with sniper / bundle flag detection: when launch buyers still hold a large share, the dump is yet to come. When they’ve already sold, you’re behind smart money. Real measured concentration — no estimates.

Exit Liquidity Calculator

How much can you actually sell

Slippage tiers from $100 to $20K against the live LP, computed with the constant-product AMM formula. Color-coded: green for safe exits, yellow for tax, red for “you’d crash the price”. Tells you the size you can realistically dump before you ape in.

AI Verdict Synthesis

Plain English, not jargon

Gemini-powered explanation of why the verdict landed where it did, in 2-4 sentences. References the actual data: dev wallet history, holder concentration, last-hour flow. Explains the mechanism (“coordinated wallets bought together at launch and now control a large share”), not just the verdict.

Foldable Sections

Read at your pace

Critical Flags, AI Verdict, Security strip, Critical Actors, Deep Analysis, Source Breakdown — every section is independently collapsible. Power users keep everything open; quick scans collapse to just the verdict + critical flags.
